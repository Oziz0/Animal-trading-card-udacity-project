Arctic Fox

Arctic Fox does not hibernate and their fur changes colours with the seasons.

  • Scientfic Name : Vulpes lagopus

  • Diet: generally eat any small animal they can find, including lemmings, voles, other rodents,ets..

  • Averge lifespan: 3 or 4 years

  • Habitat:In the summer, the arctic fox lives in the tundra at the edge of forest. In the winter, it is found on ice floes where its white coat serves as camouflage. Its den is usually a burrow in a hillside or a bank.

Creamy white fur, with a beautiful brown eyes and a fluffy white tail.
